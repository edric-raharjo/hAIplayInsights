How K-Means Works

  1. Initialize: Randomly select K points as initial cluster centers.
  2. Assign: Assign each data point to the nearest cluster center.
  3. Update: Recalculate cluster centers as the mean of assigned points.
  4. Repeat: Steps 2-3 until centers stop moving (convergence).
