What You're Learning

Classification is a supervised learning task where you learn to predict a category (bomb or not bomb) based on input features (visual patterns in images).

By manually labeling images and comparing with ground truth, you understand:

  • Ground Truth: The correct labels we compare against
  • Accuracy: Percentage of correct predictions
  • Confusion Matrix: How your predictions align with reality
  • Feature Recognition: What visual clues distinguish categories
